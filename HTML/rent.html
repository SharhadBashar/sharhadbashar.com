"The Jake" stands as Waterloo's latest symbol of luxury living, a mid-rise architectural gem comprising only 106 units. Located from uptown Waterloo, this contemporary masterpiece combines modern design with premium amenities. The building features a sleek, urban facade that complements its surroundings. Residents are greeted by a meticulously designed lobby lounge, creating a sophisticated entrance to their new home. The exterior is surrounded by professionally landscaped areas with an irrigation system, adding a touch of greenery and tranquility. The community mailboxes with a large parcel receiving area emphasize convenience for residents. For those with an active lifestyle, "The Jake" offers a contemporary Peloton studio/gym, providing a state-of-the-art fitness experience within the building. This mid-rise haven strikes the perfect balance between urban living and a serene environment, providing residents with a luxurious retreat in the heart of Waterloo.

Step into the pinnacle of contemporary living with this 1-bedroom + den unit at "The Jake."
